Our Story

The Problem We Solve

Every year, millions of farmers lose significant portions of their crops to diseases that could have been prevented or treated if detected early. Traditional methods of disease identification often require expert knowledge that isn’t readily available to farmers in remote areas.

We recognized that technology could bridge this gap. By combining artificial intelligence with agricultural expertise, we created Agripred to democratize access to crop disease detection and treatment recommendations.

Our platform empowers farmers to take immediate action, potentially saving entire harvests and securing their livelihoods.
